Recipes according to St. Hildegard of Bingen

Energy Cookie, eat only a few per day, they are indeed very strong!
"These cookies take all the bitterness out of your heart and calm your nerves. They open your heart and your five senses, provide for a good mood and cleanse your senses; reduce all bad juices, provide for good blood quality and make you productive and strong."

Spelt flour, butter, sugar, 2 eggs, a dash of salt, ground cinnamon, ground nutmeg, ground cloves, sweet almonds.
