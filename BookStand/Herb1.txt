Healing Flask
A modified version of St. Hildegards parsley wine.
Against heart failure, weakness, wounds, heart pain, infections in combination with  spice mix.  The drink improves and strenghtens health and well being, therefore you may offer it to your friends as a tasty aperitif. 
    8 – 10 parsley leaves with stems.
    red or white wine
    a splash of wine vinegar
	st. Johns wort
    half a cup of honey
