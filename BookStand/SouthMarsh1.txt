Tamadur the Butcher was dead, his army defeated, his now leaderless men on the run. But Lothar, not ready to give the enemy a chance to rally, immediately ordered the attack to crush the enemy. Meanwhile, Abdul decides to seize the moment and deal the deathblow to King Krakus VI exhausted troops. He had to succeed in disrupting their supplies, then the victory could no longer be taken away from him.
