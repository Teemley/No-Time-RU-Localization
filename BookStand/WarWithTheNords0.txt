War with the Nords I

...The barbaric Nords came by the pass way from the north of Mount Gotha, to attack Lechwia. Taking the fort of Gothburg during the harsh winter of AD 1244. The nords overcame the moutnains of Waldruna but didn't dare cross the great strôm river. They raised the fortress of Tûr Glas Teallach to the ground.
