War with the Nords II

Autumn 1253. The miners of the blue mountains report to King Krakus VI the discovery of a new ore. The Orichalcum, also known as Ark Ore, known in antiquity but lost to mere myth and stories by then. Strong and deadly blades were made out of ark alloy signaled a truning point in the war! In the years of war Lechwia was severy weakened, Ard Vuran and it's southern mercenerys took the opportunity to attack Lechwia from the south, besieging its allied port city of Tor Purperum.
