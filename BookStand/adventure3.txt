Finally our young hero was asked by Eduardo to escort him northwards to an unknown island. Just after sunrise on June 25, 1512, the two adventurers landed on the south side of the island. Once there he gave him a set of prophetical words he should follow. "This island is not known by any man yet. You have to settle here after saving your princess Eleanor, no sooner! Good luck!" With that the old man with a sailors hat went off into a cave and with a flash of lightning he was gone. Who or what he really was may never be known, but as it turned out the old man was right.
