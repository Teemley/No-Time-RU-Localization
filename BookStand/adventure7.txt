Redbeard did his best to defend against oncoming northern footmen, they laid bare as they were visible from both sides. Finally arrows pierced Redbeard and he fell to the ground. The princess and the young hero cried over him, as they said their last goodbyes over their old friend. It all seemed hopeless, as the enemy looked up at the sky. The northmen had brought flying ships with rolling thunder. As they sat there, not understanding what was going on around them, the lechwian calvary halted before them.
