Anti-gravity is a recurring concept in science fiction. Up to recently thought as a dream of the far future.
Nowadays a part of our common lives, how did the invention of this magical machine come to be?

Глава 1: Biefeld-Brown Effect
The "Biefeld–Brown effect" was named after Thomas Townsend Brown who observed an enigmatic lifting force whilst experimenting with X-ray tubes during the 1920s. With the application of voltage to an asymmetrical capacitor. Originally thought as some sort of antigravity, the word electrogravitics was coined as a result. The actual explanation was that the air would ionize between the two electrodes, moving and creating thrust.
